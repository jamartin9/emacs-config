* cooking
** recipes
- microwave baked potato
  - stab with fork all sides
  - cook for 4 minutes per side flipping once
    - add 1 min per extra potato ie 3 potatoes ~12m (6 each side)
  - split add butter, cheese, bacon, salt, pepper
- microwave green beans
  - add quarter stick of butter (no not drain)
  - microwave for 1 minute, stir and then 1 more minute.
- oven pork chop
  - 25 mins at 425
- brew tea in boiling water for 3-5 mins (darker takes longer)
- hard boiled egg
  - 10 minutes in boiling water
  - add eggs first for even cooking
  - shock in cold water after
